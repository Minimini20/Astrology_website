Astrology

Astrology is an ancient Indian art that extends way beyond your personal horoscopes. In ancient Indian religious system astrology is said to be eyes of religion. The movement and positions of celestial bodies can have a profound impact on your present, future life and everything connected to you. If one understands the patterns of the universe, it helps to understand the insight you need to navigate life.
A major part of Astrology is using the movements (transits) and relationships (aspects) of the planets to forecast what will be happening in our lives. Together with your astrology birth chart (planets position at the time of birth) we can tell about astrological events that can help you to better understand current and future opportunities.
